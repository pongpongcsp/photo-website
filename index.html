About

A photographer working between streets, faces, and weather.

Based in Taipei, I make quiet photographic studies of cities, light, distance, and the small pauses that happen between movement.

This portfolio is arranged as a restrained visual archive: minimal text, careful spacing, and images given enough room to hold attention.
